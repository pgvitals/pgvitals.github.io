Table & Index Size Ranking in PostgreSQL

Largest objects ranked by total, heap, index, and TOAST size.

What to look for

indexes_size >> heap_size (over-indexed); unexpected size growth.

The diagnostic query

SELECT
    schemaname,
    tablename,
    pg_size_pretty(
        pg_total_relation_size(schemaname || '.' || tablename)
    )                                                                  AS total_size,
    pg_size_pretty(
        pg_relation_size(schemaname || '.' || tablename)
    )                                                                  AS heap_size,
    pg_size_pretty(
        pg_indexes_size(schemaname || '.' || tablename)
    )                                                                  AS indexes_size,
    pg_size_pretty(
        pg_total_relation_size(schemaname || '.' || tablename)
        - pg_relation_size(schemaname || '.' || tablename)
        - pg_indexes_size(schemaname || '.' || tablename)
    )                                                                  AS toast_size
FROM pg_tables
WHERE schemaname NOT IN ('pg_catalog', 'information_schema')
ORDER BY pg_total_relation_size(schemaname || '.' || tablename) DESC
LIMIT 30;

How to fix it

Investigate large objects; review index necessity.
