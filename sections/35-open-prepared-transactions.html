Open Prepared Transactions in PostgreSQL

Uncommitted prepared transactions (2PC/two-phase commit).

What to look for

Any row older than 5 minutes (blocks vacuum, holds locks)

The diagnostic query

SELECT
    gid,
    prepared,
    owner,
    database,
    now() - prepared                                                          AS age,
    transaction::text                                                          AS xid
FROM pg_prepared_xacts
ORDER BY prepared ASC;

How to fix it

Run COMMIT PREPARED '<gid>'; or ROLLBACK PREPARED '<gid>';.
